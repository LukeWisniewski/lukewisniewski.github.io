Luke Wisniewski

I am a fourth year undergraduate student at Dartmouth College double majoring in economics and mathematics. I am driven by taking detailed quantiative information and applying it to interesting social questions. I have done research in Dartmouth Economics and Mathematics Mathematics departments, as well as the Tuck School of Business at Dartmouth. I recently completed a summer internship at the ifo Institute for Economic Research in Munich, Germany in the Labor and Demographics Center.

For my research in economics, I received funding through the Dartmouth Presidential Scholars Program, working under Dr. Melinda Petre. Additionally, I am an active member of the Dartmouth Economic Research Scholars program, currently serving as treasurer of the group. My research in the Math department was funded through Mellam Family Foundation, in conjunction with Dartmouth Undergraduate Research (UGR).
